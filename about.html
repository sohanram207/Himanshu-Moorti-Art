About Our Legacy

Three generations of marble craftsmanship

Our Story

For over 50 years, Marble Moorti Shop has been creating divine art that brings spirituality into homes and temples across the world.

Founded in 1970 by Master craftsman Shri Ram Sharma, our workshop has been passed down through three generations, each adding their own expertise while maintaining the traditional techniques that make our moortis truly special.

We believe that each marble sculpture carries the devotion and skill of our artisans, creating not just decorative pieces, but spiritual companions for your sacred spaces.
